Lasagna Recipe

THis is Odins special homemade lasgna recipe with a meaty, madefrom-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Ground beef
  • Onion: diced
  • Tomato sauce: seasoned
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles: Lasagna
  • Cheese: Mozzarella, paremesan
  • Eggs
  1. Prepare meat: Cook ground beef in skillet until browned and crumbly, add ionion and cook till translucscent. Stir in canned tomato sauce, half of parsley, garlic, and basil, 1.5 tsp of salt, oregano and sugar.
  2. Cook noodles in lightly salted water until al dente
  3. Make cheese layer, mix cottage cheese, parmesage cheese
  4. Assemble and bake, layer ingredients and then cover with foil and bake in oven for half an hour.
